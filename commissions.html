2. Submit request via the form. Please have references, I am not offering design services, only art and rig. If accepted I will contact you with pricing.

Process (art + rig)

1. Please read terms.
2. Submit request via the form. Please have references, I am not offering design services, only art and rig. If accepted I will contact you with pricing.
3. Payment for art portion will be made through Paypal before I start the piece. If you cancel the commission after it has started I can only provide a partial refund (50% of initial payment). After art is completed there will be no refunds on this payment.
4. I will send a sketch progress update, you may request changes at this stage. Feel free to request updates and changes while I am working on the piece. After the piece is completed only minor changes can be made.
5. After art is completed you will submit payment for rigging. I will rig head xyz, body xz, breathing, mouth, eye, physics (hair, eye, clothes), and 3 basic expressions (you pick expressions).
4. Full process for art + rig is around 3 months. If you need help setting it up on Vtube Studio I can advise, but am not familiar with other programs like Prpr.
