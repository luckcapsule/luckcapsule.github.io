Process (art only)

1. Please read terms.
2. Submit request via the form. Please have references, I am not offering design services, only art and rig. If accepted I will contact you with pricing.
3. Payment for half of full price will be made through Paypal or Stripe before I start the piece. The other half will be paid at completion before delivery of files. If you cancel the commission after it has started I can only provide a partial refund (50% of initial payment).
4. I will send regular progress updates as I work on the art, you may request changes at this stage. After the piece is completed only minor changes can be made.
5. Full process for art is around 2 months or less depending on workload. When it is complete you will provide the other half of the payment and I will send your file (.psd).

Process (art + rig)

1. Please read terms.
2. Submit request via the form. Please have references, I am not offering design services, only art and rig. If accepted I will contact you with pricing.
3. Payment for half of full price will be made through Paypal or Stripe before I start the piece. If you cancel the commission after it has started I can only provide a partial refund (50% of initial payment). After art is completed there will be no refunds on this payment.
4. I will send regular progress updates as I work on the art, you may request changes at this stage. After the piece is completed only minor changes can be made.
5. After art is completed you will submit the second and final payment before the rigging starts.
6.I will continue to send updates throughout the rig, as with before you can request changes as we go. Only minor changes can be made once it is complete (without additional charge)
7. Full process for art + rig is around 3 months or less depending on workload. You will receive a .png and files necessary for running the model. The .psd can be provided upon request. The .cmo3 file will only be provided with additional charge (see above). I can assist with setup when it is complete!
